Game Enemies

Lemon Monsters

Found in: Forest

Angry lemon beasts with health bars. Each monster drops 1 lemon when defeated. They spawn at the lemon hide and guard their citrus treasures fiercely.

Drops: 🍋 1 Lemon Health: ❤️ Health Bar Attack: 🦵 Kick

Sugar Cane

Found in: Beach

A peaceful, non-aggressive creature that doesn't attack players. Despite its gentle nature, it must be harvested for its valuable sugar. Feels wrong, but necessary for your lemonade quest.

Drops: 🍬 1 Sugar Health: ❤️ Health Bar Attack: ❌ None (Peaceful)

Ice Slimes

Found in: Ice Cave

Bouncy ice creatures that slide around the cave floors. They attack by launching themselves at players and multiply when hit.

Drops: ❄️ 1 Ice Health: ❤️ Health Bar Attack: 💥 Body Slam

Hailcrusher (BOSS)

Found in: Ice Cave Depths

The massive ice demon that controls the cave. Shoots ice projectiles and creates devastating blizzard attacks. The ultimate challenge!

Drops: ❄️ Boss Loot Health: ❤️ Health Bar Attack: ❄️ Ice Projectiles
