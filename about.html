Our Idea

How We Picked Our Idea

When brainstorming game concepts, we wanted something fun and unique that would make people smile. The phrase "when life gives you lemons, make lemonade" sparked our creativity - but we thought, what if death gave you lemons instead?

We loved the idea of turning a simple lemonade stand into an epic adventure. Instead of just buying ingredients, our heroes Joe and Jade have to hunt monsters and explore dangerous locations to gather what they need. It's the perfect blend of wholesome business goals and exciting monster-hunting action!

The concept let us create diverse gameplay - from combat in the forest to puzzle-solving in beach mazes to boss battles in icy caves. Plus, who doesn't love the idea of making the perfect lemonade after an epic quest?
