Character Design Philosophy

When designing Joe and Jade, we wanted to create characters that felt approachable and relatable while still being capable heroes. We chose simple, clean designs that would work well in our pixel art style and be easily recognizable during gameplay. Both characters share the same abilities to emphasize their partnership in running the lemon stand together. Their designs reflect everyday people who've been thrust into an extraordinary situation - they're not traditional fantasy heroes, just two friends trying to save their small business through monster hunting adventures.
